About me...

Howdy! I'm Charles Milam. I am a native Texian, raised in Austin, where I currently live.

I am a recent graduate of MakerSquare, Cohort 10. MakerSquare is a 3 month full-time career accelerator for software developers. By teaching computer science fundamentals and modern web technologies, MakerSquare prepares students to join the workforce as full-stack web developers. We concentrated on Javascript, jQuery, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails. We had exposure to, and worked with, Javascript frameworks Mithril and React. We also made use of Sinatra and CSS frameworks Bootstrap and Foundation.

I am seeking an opportunity as a juinor level web developer. I am particularly interested in back-end development, especially with Rails, but also looking to gain experience and expand my knowledge in the front-end, and other back-end technologies. The MEAN stack and Meteor I find very interesting, and plan to explore those technologies on my own.

I am a lifelong learner, and have taken recent courses on Python and MongoDB, as well as a variety of MOOCs for subjects I wanted to learn more about outside of software development. I am a subscriber to CodeSchool, and have an active profile you can see here.
